Introduction
Exo is a geometric sans serif font that was designed to work well as both a display font and work in smaller sized texts. It presents a futuristic design all while maintaing a elegant and legible style. The font has 9 weights and an italic version of each. It was updated in December of 2013 adding Exo 2 further refining the design looking more organic while performing better at a smaller scale. This was designed by Natanael Gama. Note this site uses Exo 2.
